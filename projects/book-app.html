XD Daily Challenge - Buy a Book, Give a Book

This is an independent project that I worked on as part of Adobe’s Daily Creative Challenge. The objective of this challenge was to create a donation experience for a ‘buy a book, give a book’ mobile app.

I chose simple colors and shapes for this app to provide a clean and enjoyable experience. I also integrated rounded corners and content displayed in modals since this is a modern design trend.
