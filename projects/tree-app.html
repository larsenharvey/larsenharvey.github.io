XD Daily Challenge - Tree Planting App

This is an independent project that I worked on as part of Adobe’s Daily Creative Challenge. The objective of this challenge was to create a mobile experience that tracks home or business utility usage. This challenge focused on advanced prototyping and animation techniques.

I created smooth water-like animations when hiding or showing the user’s water utility usage, and stuck to simple colors. I also integrated Neumorphism into the top buttons, as this is a very recent design trend.
