XD Daily Challenge - Tree Planting App

This is an independent project that I worked on as part of Adobe’s Daily Creative Challenge. The objective of this challenge was to create a digital experience that rewards users with credits for planting trees in the real world.

I chose a uniform color scheme as well as fonts and shapes that mimic the playful nature of the app experience. Users are able to view their current progress and compare it to the progress of their friends through a horizontal menu slider. Users are also able to purchase gift cards with their tokens as an incentive.
